# General Player Guidelines

## Trust & Permissions

* Only add people you **actually trust** to your team.
* If someone has access → they can interact with your base.

## Claim Your Base

* Always keep your base **claimed**.
* Unclaimed base = not protected.
* Your items = your responsibility.

## Supporting Modded Together

* Check the support channel on Discord.
* Pick a tier → message **Pitersonix** → perks apply.
