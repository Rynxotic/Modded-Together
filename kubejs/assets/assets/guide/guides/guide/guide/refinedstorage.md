# Refined Storage

## Getting Started

You will require:

- A power source, such as an alternator from create,
- A controller to accept power into the system, <ItemImage id='refinedstorage:controller'/>
- A grid to be able to access item, <ItemImage id='refinedstorage:grid'/>
- Any form of storage(Chests, Item Vault) or a disk drive with disks inside.<ItemImage id='refinedstorage:disk_drive'/>

__Building the System__

* Place down the controller,
* Place down the grid ontop of the controller,
* Place the alternator going into the back of the controller,
* Place down Disk Drive and Insert Drives OR/AND Connect Storage via External Storage Cable

After this is done you will have a fully working storage system.
